Owner's Story

After years of honing his craft as a professional chef, Eric McGraw founded The Blackboard Kart with a clear vision: to bring exceptional food and genuine connections to the community. Eric’s lifelong passion for cooking goes beyond creating delicious dishes — he thrives on sharing stories and building relationships through the universal language of food and conversation. With a commitment to quality and a love for bringing people together, The Blackboard Kart serves up more than just meals; it serves memorable experiences.
