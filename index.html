Owner's Story

After years of honing his craft as a professional chef working at the Logan Tavern, Eric McGraw founded The Blackboard Kart with a clear vision: to bring exceptional food and genuine connections to the community. Eric’s lifelong passion for cooking goes beyond creating delicious dishes — he thrives on sharing stories and building relationships through the universal language of food and conversation. With a commitment to quality and a love for bringing people together, Eric not only uses his kart to connect with the community, But he also works alongside his family, spending quality time with them while pursuing his passion. The Blackboard Kart serves up more than just meals; it serves memorable experiences.

Meet the Chef

Chef Eric McGraw is a passionate culinary artist who believes food is a powerful way to connect with people. With years of experience at the Logan Tavern in downtown Spokane, Eric’s love for cooking and community inspired him to open The Blackboard Kart. When he’s not crafting delicious dishes, Eric enjoys spending quality time with his wife and two sons, cherishing the moments that make life flavorful both inside and outside the kitchen.
